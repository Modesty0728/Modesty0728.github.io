Topic: War (Maritimes)

Rhyme scheme: AA BB CC DD

Type of the first poetry: Narrative Poetry

the Flower of the War

I’m the only color of the war,

the beauty the world chased for;

But I’m crying in the dark,

with human blood saturating my heart;

The dazzle upon me refers to fallacies,

satirizing nothing but casualties.

I’m fiery incompatible with surroundings’ cruel feature,

indicating the dripping blood of culture.

Type of the second poetry: Free Verse

Nanjing, Nanjing

You are the Capital City of six dynasties,

burdened too much sadness;

Once scarred and battered,

you withstood enemies although injured;

Prosperity disappeared simultaneously,

beyond which new life thrived rapidly.

Arguably you are invincible,

for standing up in the blood with the hopeful symbol.
