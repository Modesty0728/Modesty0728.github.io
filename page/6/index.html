Stay Humble And Be Full

Today I watched a film called Hi, mom with my family, a great screen work in terms of appreciation of mother’s love which makes us wildly laugh with tears stand on the rim of eye. I think the morals are really meaningful which can be inferred from several scenes.

One of the classical scene is about the volleyball battle between Li Huanying and Wang Qin. There are tensions between the two girls so they are both eager to defeat each other. And the furious players are outraged even more when it comes to match point. However, I was so much impressed when Huanying said, “Don’t be serious with the match. Just consider it as a game!” I appreciate Huanying’s tolerance and calming mentality. She is brave enough to put winning and losing aside. Due to her outstanding magnanimity, Wang Qin has already been beaten completely.

When Shen Zhilin is performing comet in front of the audience, he and Jia Xiaoling are very amazed at the calming audience since their friend Zhang Jiang can’t stop laughing during the rehearsal. This particular scene remind me of the different opinions among generals and comet effects on stage and from rehearsal. So when we want to create a particular atmosphere for a show, reactions from different groups of people should be collected in order to avoid embarrassing occasions in a performance.

The subsequent reality caters to my satisfaction. Wang Qin, snobby and arrogant towards Li Huanying, flaunts her son’s relative success to Huanying pridefully. That is exactly why Jia Xiaoling does her best to help Huanying lead a better life instead of abasing herself. Nevertheless, it is very dramatic to notice that Jia Xiaoling becomes a well-known celebrity in the future. Huanying says, “I only want a healthy and happy kid, rather than a renowned superstar.” Even if Xiaoling is not so successful, she and Huanying are absolutely more cheerful and light-hearted than Wang’s family. There’s no need to compare ourselves with each other. Live happily, life meaningful!

The most moving scene in the movie is the young Li Huanying with the real mind of the dying old Huanying answers Jia when Jia murmurs “mom”, which is also the impressive climate of the whole movie. Usually, time-travel series’s most regrettable thing is the impossibility of other characters to realize the time-traveler’s real identity. But in Hi, mom, the climax surprises all of us which turn the impossibility into something possible, breaking the barrier of time.

I think Huanying would never regret to have Xiaoling as her daughter no matter what kind of fate she will face, because she asks for nothing.

Someone Is Suitable to Stay In 2020

“Maybe someone is suitable to stay in 2020.”

When I first saw this piece of comment, it seems the only layer of meaning is： I’d better stay in this year to burden pains over and over again to stay strong in order to meet cruel society in future. But today, which is also the last day of 2020 in lunar year, eventually I discover something else that it is implying. It is someone in my memory that should stay in 2020 indeed, rather than myself. However, I would not deny their existence, but generally “let it go”, which is in the likeness of dust covered by fallen leaves.

However, we are supposed to move on. Someone once said, “The flowing water is competing for lasting gush rather than speed”. Li Bai, the genius poet in Tang Destiny, exclaims: “Send word to songbirds: stop quarreling and fly towards sky, which is thousands of miles high. “ My dear, friends, experience the sensation of running forward wildly, cuz only in this way can we reach the peak of the mountain of life, having a great view of unknown panorama.

someone is suitable to stay in 2020.

Thank you for these living-beings’ company, but you will never be the one who makes me better.

​ write in the end of 2020 (lunar year)

find it useful to study Shakspeare on the first day

Yesterday I started reading the first tale from Shakspeare, namely the Tempest. To my surprise, the grammar and vocabulary are not so difficult to understand while I learned several useful phrases and expressions. I like the appearance description of Caliban: “far less human in form than an ape” and some long sentences like: “By his orders they raised a violent storm, in the midst of which, and struggling with the wild sea-waves that every moment threatened to swallow it up, he showed his daughter a fine large ship, which he told her was full of living beings like themselves.” Shakspeare intended to use a variety of complicated long sentences which are quite hard to understand, but it is also very interesting to analyze them and absorb the writing skills. The first two pages I read chiefly treat of the background information of the island and main characters.

be quiet, and alone

Many people clarify that team work makes success, but through yesterday’s satisfying studies, I deeply realize the strong power of repeated self-thinking. Before, I enjoy meditation which fosters philosophical thoughts being came up with. Recently I’m struggling with Microeconomics and just cannot understand those abstract concepts, such as marginal utility, consumer surplus and dead weight loss. Nevertheless, I was really surprised to find out that persevering in trying to understand finally could work. Self-reflection On Winter Syrup

I choose this topic because when I was reading and analyzing O! Me! O! Life! I started thinking about my own verse of contribution and immediately my eager of being nice to others came into my mind because it is exactly my true deep feeling. I think the question raised by the topic is quite important to me since the question helps me put my own experiences and feelings into deeper consideration and comprehension. There are loads of revision of this piece of work because it is the first time I work on a slam poetry. To make it achieve the standard of slam poetry, I actually spent loads of time creating rhyme scheme, repetition and looking for slang. The whole poem demonstrates my own thoughts: winter syrup represents both the speaker’s feeling when getting along with others and the speaker herself while to some extent the contributions of the speaker (represents the real me) and winter syrup are same, so the speaker’s contribution to this world is actually herself (it is actually a equivalent substitution in Math). And for these few lines:

Winter syrup flows inside body:

carefully creeping on my tongue,

lightheartedly running in my stomach,

easefully disappearing in the end.

I just want to show that the speaker feels sweet like having the syrup before, while and after she shows kindness to others. The sweet feeling is divided into three stages in the order of time, e.g. “carefully creeping” and “tongue” means the state which is before the speaker shows kindness. Also, in the poem I show the speaker has a thinking period:

Tik tock. Tik tock.

Dripping of syrup sounds familiar!

Tik tock. Tick tock.

Dripping of syrup compares with moving pointer of clock.

I do not want my readers think that the speaker is so smart that she could get any truth without struggling. “Tik tock” and “clock” here indicate the speaker is thinking and linking syrup to herself. And I add “winter” before “syrup” because in this way the warmth and happiness of syrup can be better emphasized:

True warmth comes in winter,

’tis the season

when I become meaningful.
