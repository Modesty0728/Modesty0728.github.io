​ Akron Ohio

​ December 4th, 2081

Dear family,

​ Umm…hi…

​ Do you miss me? I suppose you still remember me…But to be honest, the eighteenth birthday of a tithe is not so significant right? After all you always told me: high God love you much more than we do.

Gradually I blocked my fabulous imagination towards jobs and marriage, and my mind had nothing but “God is mighty”. MAYBE I SHOULD BE THANKFUL FOR YOU GAVE ME A SPECIAL SHELTERED STRUCTURE TO GROW UP? Don’t you think it sounds ridiculous and familiar, like what Snape asks Dumbledore in their last talking: You’ve been raising Harry Potter like a pig for slaughter? I know, I know, irritably you would blurt out: how can you say that? The pig is nothing, but you are nearest to holy God! Gosh how could you…My fault, you have definitely been thinking in that way since I was born, and…I also find this one tricky. A few days ago, I told another unwind living without purpose is dreadful and all that in my mind forcing me to turn him in is your voice— “why not “contribute a voice to the world instead of being so meaningless?” But what’s your “holy” comprehension for “meaningless”? Uh, God! Because of your damn faith, I hurt the boy, and myself! For thirteen years, YOU ARE ALWAYS THE DIMNESS OF FIRE! I’m arrogant, exclusive, acting like cold-blooded animals, all blame on you. You still find yourself righteous? Read on. After I escaped from cops I called, I realize how much I want to be alive! In the past I was not alone due to the “glorious solace”, however now I am, indeed, separating myself from the identity of tithe. It is a brand-new feeling which I did not have, or in other words, should not have when being with you, like a fresh spring washing a headful of rust away. And for the first time I’m scared, conspicuously. Ha, levi, finally regretful? You wish. I want to be alive— totally alive. Eventually I start learning how to be a living person.

Curiously, how can loneliness, an emotion as cold as me, transform me and warm up my heart? Tell you then. Everyone is born with a warm heart and gets company mostly. I’m envious about these companions in the likeness of forest fawns continuously transporting wood for the burning of soul, because care from you “saints” are just water vapor forming THE DIMNESS, so heavy actually, far beyond what I can burden. Indeed, you keep me company but what you will never realize is that the more you accompany me (actually brainwash me), the more I get to suffer, and MY SOUL ON FIRE is more and more invisible. Clearly, now you can see the different function of “wood” and “water vapor”.

​ I was stifling under water! But what else can I blames on you…You were fiercely protective towards me…maybe just as a tithe…I understand…I wouldn’t choose myself either…I cannot be more frustrated than now. Thirteen years of brainwashing…I will never forgive you…Have you ever respected my soul??? My soul comes to this world for living, rather than some ludicrous mission!

​ Nevertheless… am I correct…? Who is correct about this world? Pastor Dan? Me? You? Jesus…my brain is breaking into pieces… Maybe you are correct…maybe…If so, I become tiny water living in sponge while your “justice” is the hand squeezing me out… I have my entire body on your hand whereas will never be noticed and then go by the wind…uh then become “vapor” ha… a further step away from “myself”. Perhaps I’m just too selfish…or wouldn’t be so painful towards this…Is Pastor Dan doing the right thing—Let me run away? I don’t know but I do feel relieved…like being saved from death. So…let me be selfish…just for one time…okay? I haven’t found “myself” yet. I need more time definitely and I’m not in a hurry. But I do see the hazy figure of myself deep down. He wants to be alive, truly. Why so desirable? No idea. But please…this time let me follow my heart…and I won’t blame on you anymore…I need to figure out who I am. Others are just meaningless to me now.

Besides, I haven’t loved myself for thirteen years.

​ Your TREASURE,

​ Lev Calder

Trust

Today I watched a recent Disney film Raya and the Last Dragon. That really really touches me. Firstly, its theme, about trust, is very original. Moreover, it makes me recall my own experience: I was also hurt so many times and dare not to trust anymore.

Sometimes, we are hurt by someone we trust. The feeling of being betrayed if like the burning of heart. Just like Raya’s father, Sisu, and Raya herself. I quite understand that the feeling is strong and stifling enough to destroy someone’s fantasy forever, and what is most difficult is to offer another chance to build the bridge of trust. Step first, and you will gain something out of imagination.

Even if you are hurt by this world from head to toes, please still hold your kindness, because the world would always reward you.

Have you ever been defined as some kind of person you don’t like? I do. You know, I’m born introverted but don’t want to make others uncomfortable.

I feel like a fish struggling beneath The Arctic Ocean, envious about strong waves breaking up ice. My junior high classmates define me as a girl who is “boy-proof”. Everyone, even some of my good friends, joking about this from time to time. “I don’t want to be her desk-mate because she is so dour!” “I don’t know her…wait a minute, Uh I see… She is the one never talks.” “You know, my friend Modesty, she never talks to a boy!” Now it seems waves are teasing me while drawing me into a huge vortex they formed. The darkness intensifies my fear towards myself, making it tougher to break the icy layer. I feel so sorry to them that I try to use my miserableness to exchange their awkwardness and force myself to speak out, to change. But nothing happens and I define myself a waste of space. All of them are trying their best attacking the injury that I struggle to hide…and they succeed. Time gradually washes memories away, and I’m so cheerful to graduate. Entering a brand-new environment, I feel so great to define myself again. And one day, I casually talks to a psychologist who is a friend of my family’s about my “hidden defect”. Simultaneously she has tears falling down, and I’m totally confused. She takes my hand, with tears in eyes, whispering: “My girl, the only reason that gets you a ‘defect’, is the lack of your father’s company. That’s why you don’t know how to get along with boys.” At that moment, I’m relieved, but then heartbroken. So I haven’t done anything wrong but only my plush toys know how miserable I am. I really want to hug that little girl and tell her “It’s okay.” But I don’t want to end up this now. I won’t be inclined to self-pity anymore, because no one can define me, no one can decide who I am anymore. I will always believe in myself rather than being humble towards worthless people. What doesn’t kill me makes me stronger. Next time, please watch your tongue, because your rumors are ridiculous in front of a strong girl.

unwind part3 questions

Part THREE:

u Why does Hayden call the harvest camps “black holes”? Is the metaphor an appropriate one? (pg 80)

\1. A black hole is a region of spacetime where nothing—no particles or even electromagnetic radiation such as light—can escape from it. According to Hayden, no one knows what happens in the harvest camp only the final result of being unwound. In other words, no one exactly knows the process of unwinding and the treatments on unwinds.

\2. The metaphor is quite appropriate because it clearly tells us how mysterious the harvest camp and the unwinding process is and also emphasizes that this unknown place is some kind of horrible, connecting to this novel’s writing topic—dystopia.

u Who is Humphrey Dunfree? Do you think his story is truth, fiction, or something in-between? (pgs 81-82)

\1. He is the “survivor” of unwind order. Like other kids, Humphrey get what he “deserved” after his parents sign the order who however, going mad towards it. Then they hacked in to The National Unwind Database, getting to know each Humphrey’s single part’s new owners and secretly searching them to paying what they owed to Humphrey back in the way of rearrangement.

\2. I think his story is something between truth and fiction. According to the previous context, a computer virus diminished Connor’s discussion towards Humphrey. I don’t think it is a coincidence or the government wouldn’t be so stressed out and furious. However, Humphrey’s story is also too uncanny to believe so I suppose some parts of it is made up by imagination or exaggeration, but it must be based on the real event. No matter the story is one-hundred percent realistic or not, it’s quite sure that in this mad world parents’ guilty emotions exist and there are some crazy actions to take trying to turn the unwound result into reversible. It clearly shows the fragility of human nature and people are struggling against the dystopia.

u Why does Sonia have the Unwinds write letters home? What does Connor learn from his letter writing experience? (pg 85)

\1. Sonia wants the unwinds to write down everything not being said before to someone they love. And the letters will be sent once they reached eighteen.

(a. For the unwinds,

is a good chance for releasing themselves from the miserable memories. Writing letters also helps them recognize themselves’ true attitudes towards everything, including family, unwind order, and the crazy world. No matter what would happen in the next, at least the unwinds take up the responsibility of speaking up for themselves and the people who are suffering in dystopia. The unwinds have already made a contribution on behalf of themselves to the world. Sonia makes the unwinds’ “useless” life meaningful.

Self-writing also helps them relieved with no regret left.

(b. For their guardians,

No matter their kids would survive or not, the guardians would receive the letter on the unwinds’ eighteenth birthday. Sonia chooses this special day for certain reasons. It is like the guardians push their kids into water without looking back, and one day they see the same pool however with no water and simultaneously are sent a previous video about how the stifling kids were struggling beneath water without knowing whether the poor kids survive or not. When guardians receive letter, there are not danger for the unwinds anymore. However, the easeful status-quo would definitely get them realized how gloomy the hell that they have put kids into is. As aforementioned, it’s just like watching kids suffering from over head water when there’s no water remains anymore. Objectively, Sonia aims to get the guardians at least been aware of how miserable the kids are under the unwind order. And subjectively, she wants the guardians to respect their kids’ thoughts by reading letters and reflect on themselves about their recognition towards human nature. By letting the guardians know the unwinds better, there’s a slight hope that they may also contribute a voice to the world by changing the attitude towards “unwind” to gradually make less kids suffer. After all, they the ones who decide life and death to some extent.

\2. From the writing experience, Connor recognizes many things.

(a. Firstly, He doesn’t truly love Ariana because she is not the one offering hope and strength now, and that’s why Connor thinks writing to Ariana is pointless.

(b. Deep down in Connor’s heart, he still loves his parents. Connor’s sweet memories towards his family seem to be blocked once the unwind order is found. Everything his family do next is shaded with darkness which looks so hopeless that Connor’s heart begins to be full of it, which numbs his mind and makes him feel like all the memories is in darkness now. In other words, it is proved to be an illusion and the precious deep love Connor’s parents offered is still too strong and impressed to forget. That’s why Connor finds himself still love them, as a spontaneous respond to their love.

\1. Hannah is willing to take the baby, however Connor asks whether Hannah wants the baby, and then Risa wants Connor’s answer towards the question if it is set for him.

\2. Connor hesitates for a while and goes on the truck after emphasizing the baby’s gender—female. Deep down Connors knows that Hannah and him is the same. They don’t want the baby, but are willing to take it and protect it from danger. Now Connor has finished his responsibility and willingness of take care about the baby to be less guilty towards previous experience. He knows he has done his best to save her, and that’s enough.

u What’s the difference between a run-away and a run-to? Why do you think CyFi is a run-to? (pg 92)

\1. A run-to has a destination is his/her mind, which is the main difference compared with a run-away. Run-aways and run-toes are all escaping from the terrible unwind order, however the latter has a more explicit plan.

\2. We all know that in the previous context, Risa, Connor and Lev are exactly three run-aways who are always nervous and wary to deal with occasional emergencies. CyFi is completely different because he is quite relaxed walking along railway and knows exactly where he is going to although he cannot tell.

u Why does Lev feel like he’s an accomplice to murder? Why is this word choice and connotation significant? (pg 94)

\1. Because Risa and Connor now are probably and caught and then unwound, which is all lev’s fault. Lev turned the two “kidnappers” in so he feels ashamed to push them to the end of their life.

\2. (a. Firstly this word shows Connor’s point of view is changing.

(c. This word is thought-provoking. It makes us think about the nature of unwinding again. Is it murdering or not? This word not only fosters lev to think deeply ignoring his family’s ideas, but also allows readers to have their own speculations because there’s no exact answer.

u How is CyFi’s experience with Unwind parts different from everyone else we’ve read about so far? How do you think this could cause problems? (pgs 95-96)

\1. CyFi was hit when riding a hike so his right temporal lobe is widely damaged. However, instead of getting body parts or bits e.g. an arm, CyFi got an entire temporal lobe during the surgeon.

\2. Scattered body parts may have some body memories, but are individual and weak. But an entire temporal lobe means part of the entire nerve system, and individual tissues are connecting into each other. That means CyFi got a real strong and independent lobe which can influence and even control him sometimes.

u What does Lev realize on page 99? How is this significant for his character?
