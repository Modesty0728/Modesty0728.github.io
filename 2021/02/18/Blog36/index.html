sixth day with unwind

Part 1 and Part 2 finally ends up in excitement. Fortunately Risa and Connor find their temporary cover through the help of the school teacher Hannah and an old woman namely Sonia. Getting rid of the baby, Risa and Connor soon get on the way towards another temporary safe house. Here are the meaningful sentences:

“Love the ones you can,” Nurse Greta told her. “Pray for the rest.”

It’s true. Everyone has limited energy to offer even though being willing to offer heart to all people. But we do not need to blame ourselves since similar chances need saving for other desirable good man. We just need to offer our kindness to all of them, which is already quite enough.

“People aren’t all good, and people aren’t all bad. We move in and out of darkness and light all of our lives. Right now, I’m pleased to be in the light.”

Being in the light is what good person does. But as aforementioned, it is not a perfect world. It is not being in the light makes us good people, but the courage of escaping from darkness to stand in light.
