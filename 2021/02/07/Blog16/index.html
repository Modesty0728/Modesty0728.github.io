be quiet, and alone

Many people clarify that team work makes success, but through yesterday’s satisfying studies, I deeply realize the strong power of repeated self-thinking. Before, I enjoy meditation which fosters philosophical thoughts being came up with. Recently I’m struggling with Microeconomics and just cannot understand those abstract concepts, such as marginal utility, consumer surplus and dead weight loss. Nevertheless, I was really surprised to find out that persevering in trying to understand finally could work. No matter how complicated the question is, as long as going over notes over and over again, it would be solved unconsciously. We do need others’ good ideas, but enough time should also be reserved to offer ourselves a chance to get over difficulty. In this way, we build up confidence, and more importantly, we develop the spirit of persistence.
