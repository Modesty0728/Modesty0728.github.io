our real charm

Yesterday I read about a piece of motto: truly outstanding charm comes from our hearts, which is our gentleness. In the evening, when I’m selecting new year’s suits with my mother, towards whom the shopping assistant appreciated: “how tender your daughter is!” That really made me recalls last semester’s Winter Syrup, in which I expressed the intension to comfort and benefit everyone in the world. I thought my so-called gentleness is useless and always ignored until now I realize its significance: cheer others up. And most importantly, cheer myself up, gaining eternal joy.

Today’s motto: knowledge makes humble, ignorance makes proud. So please always take in others’ suggestions, demonstrating I’m thinking about life.
