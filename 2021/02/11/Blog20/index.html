My Favourite Song and Album

Recently I found a fabulous album released by Dabin in 2019, containing 10 electroacoustic works. Last summer I discovered the single called Bloom. The fresh music style of chorus immediately attracts me which sounds like bells’ slight collision along with the reverberation of flowing freezing water. Later I found myself prefer the lyrics than the beautiful tune:

“Where we are stay this way

wrapped up in a summer daze

try to find that piece of time

that let’s me freeze you in my mind

I don’t want us to change don’t want us to chip away

oh I am trying to save our bodies against the waves

could we stay in bloo-oo-oom

everything is new ooh ooh

I don’t want to leave this room just let me stay

let me stay with you

could we stay in bloo-oo-oom

could we stay in

could we stay in

could we stay in

could we stay in

holding tight to what we had

if it’s lost will it come back

tell me if this windin’road

is made of dirt or made of gold

…………”

It was exactly the gratuate season when I was listening to this song. And I thought the bloom was refering to our fine youth, which together with my friendship I afraid to chip away. I’m also crazy over the unique style of the album’s cover, drawn by Erica William. It demonstrates a group of teenagers are ready to conceur danger without any fear in a painting style much similar to Miyazaki Hayao, which fully applies colors to a drawing of Utopia.
