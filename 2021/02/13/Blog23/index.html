second day with unwind

Last time I mentioned of the relative successful escape of Connor. To my great surprise, Connor is now caught by cops and in front of him is exactly his own father, which indicating the unfortunate destiny is coming again. How can this happen? The incident is attributed to Connor’s carelessness. He has forgotten to turn off his cell phone to cut down signals in case of being tracked. That remind me of the importance of thinking thoroughly to protect myself. Connor’s dangerous current situation concerns me a lot. What he gonna do? Connor makes a split-second decision of running again in front of his father and cops, which is absolutely a very intrepid act. You know, some people can be born with a unfortunate fate. Sometimes they have the chance to convert it into miracle, however only by great courage and tenacious spirit.
