third day with unwind

Today I come to notice two more new main characters, namely Risa and Lev. Compared with Connor, Risa is not a naughty and worrisome kid, but a good however not excellent girl. Risa’s school cannot bear such normal kids so sent her to a unwind case. Risa’s world is stifling. You be the best, or nothing. It forces every kids to study desperately and the miserable fate would come if mistakes are made.

Lev is completely different from both Risa and Connor, since he is willing to be unwound as a tithe. When he is rescued by them, he pretends to work together with them while preparing to get three of them unwound in the future.

Written and placed individually in terms of main characters own perspectives, the context and plot is closely connected while showing every unwinds’ perspectives towards what’s going on.
