Superhero

In the parallel universe, some well-known mythical civilizations really exist. There’s a kingdom full of gold called El Dorado, once upon the time a girl of which fell in love with the king of Tibet, the Tibetan holy land which is quite relevant to our country, so they gave birth to our superhero–Buddha.

Naturally, this young prince of Tibet own a super mixed power as a gift for connecting the two kingdoms, which is the ability to control gold when chanting Buddhist scriptures. That’s why El Dorado’s stolen wealth can always be recaptured. But he also has a secret power unknown to the others.

And by the way, you may think Buddha has got a weird name, but there’s something extraordinary about it indeed. In Tibetan, “Buddha” refers to diamond, which reflects his good traits like frugal, tenacious and intrepid. However, he is also pathetically distant and exclusive due to the loss of mother in his infancy.

I think “superhero”, this honorary title is well deserved for Buddha takes up the duty of eliminating greed rather than enjoying the joyous and carefree life in Tibet when greedy rulers invaded El Dorado to make a fortune. And more importantly, he protects the two historic and precious civilizations by utilizing the hidden power eventually to conceal them from the world. All he has done makes him a legend.
