Outline for Marijuana

Homework: pls finish the outline of the essay topic “legalize Marijuana or not?”

Hook: In 2017, more Americans were arrested for marijuana possession than for murder, rape, aggravated assault and robbery combined. There’s one Marijuana arrest every minute.

Transition: as we all know, behaviors like theft are illegal with lower rate of arrest. So would the legalization of Marijuana truly help improve its control?

Thesis: the government should legalize Marijuana indeed

Point 1: the ban of Marijuana intensifies the intensions related to racism.

Point 2: legalizing Marijuana benefits the growth of economy

Conclusion: the legalization of Marijuana stabilizes the society

Expand: following the common sense of boycotting drugs resolutely cannot always be the justice. Sometimes human beings’ mindset need switching. It is impossible for us to measure whether the prohibition is more predominant which can also cause social unrest.
