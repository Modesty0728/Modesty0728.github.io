Debate-conclusion

Clearly there are some clashes between pro and con side. But I can tell that the it is con side that causes this situation due to your logical fallacies and vague comprehension towards nearly everything, generally five points:

\0. There are actually two ways of dealing with nuclear leak, which means that what we have all said about ocean pollution, the fisherman can be totally avoided.

\1. Nuclear energy is not as dangerous as you said—and even safer than driving a car. So why don’t you just worry about whether you should allow FP to drive a car?

\2. Nuclear power plants are not responsible for protecting native culture, since it is the cultural department in the government that should take in charge.

\3. Shut down power plants will not save the natural resources. Without provided nuclear energy, FP would do continuous logging. And it is so beneficial to the environment!

\4. Last but not least, you have mentioned what happened in Japan right? The accidents happened just because they did not inject cool water in time. So if you are not in a coastal place, how can you ensure you can offer cool water sustainably?

So, thank you so much for mentioning “Japan”, and “ocean” for so many times, providing us with so great examples to prove our thesis statement. And in conclusion, with great advantage in sustainability, economy, and humanity industry, we believe that nuclear power plants have a bright future in remote near-sea area. Nuclear power has been a significant character of the contemporary energy source, so we should catch up with the trend and keep abreast with the society. Above all, we can fully prove that it is possible to construct nuclear power plants. So, we can win the debate based on our framework.
