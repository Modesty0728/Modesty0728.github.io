Do You Think Today’s Health and Beauty Industry is Dangerous?

It depends. People from distinct religions and regions are supporting heterogeneous beauty standards. The same fact is—people are always chasing for beauty, because nearly everyone wants to have a not-so-bad look. And that is normal, very common based on human nature. So, if we only consider the perfect beauty standards, the health and beauty industry are safe, since these represent people’s nature of admiring beauty and their ideal figures. However, what we are concerning now is the methods used to approach these standards. Friends around me would instinctively laugh at my relevantly darker skin, frequently going on diets, and even plan carefully for a future plastic surgery. I’ve always been confused. Instinctively I cannot help but disapproving their manners. But why? I mean—everyone has their rights to chase for beauty, so is this also their right to take any extreme ways to achieve their goals? Maybe it is. So why am I denying their legitimate decisions? Perhaps it is due to my instinct to stick up for “natural beauty”. I even get uncomfortable when seeing my friends infatuated with make-ups. I’ve reflected on myself for so many times. Don’t be so extreme! Don’t be so harsh on your friends if yourself do not like it! You have to understand it is others’ right! Besides, even myself once planned to purchase a bunch of Dior or Channel’s cosmetics when growing into an adolescent. Literally, I enjoyed considering about that kind of thing! That made me feel confident, as if my life was extremely colorful. I can only tell that like me, preferring nature can refer to less care in personal appearance (well I still make sure that I’m in a tidy and easeful look). It is really hard for me to answer whether striving to be pretty by using whatever possible methods is correct and rational. I can only say that there is sequential safe level for health and beauty industry. The more thoughts you put on this field, the more “risks” you are likely to take since you’ve put too much precious energy on superficial fantasy. Deep down, infinite emptiness is out of ignorance because you are not living for yourself anymore but your appearance.
