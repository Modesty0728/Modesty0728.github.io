2021-12-31

a proud achievement

The unexpected academic result in Drama 10 has pushed me a big step further towards the destination of self-aware. Born to be extremely introverted, I never expected a good result on this course when hearing about it is a compulsory one. Common redemptions were even kicked away from the field of drama in advance since I kept self-telling: this was born to be your weakness. Accept it. Don’t get frustrated when you turn to be the worst student on final transcripts. Life is full of drama. Even myself cannot believe that I came first academically with 99% on average. Perhaps myself was much braver than I have ever imagined? Realization comes to me that my attention was not distracted on high but unrealistic expectations anymore—every time receiving the task the only goal I hold was to get out of the comfort zone, bit by bit because I never gave in to self-defined personal traits deep down. Initial shyness in practice videos just stimulated my unwilling naturally which boosted the quality of performances. And now I am confident to tell the cool progress: I have turned my disadvantage into advantage. Thanks to this period, a brand-new self emerges who is much stronger than expected. What I define myself as is usually what others comment on me. That could be true. However, only personal traits can be defined, other than my consequential ability only if the heart of persistence is beating.
