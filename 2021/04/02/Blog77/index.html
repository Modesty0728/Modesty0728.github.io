Mid-term Performance

Yesterday I finished my mid-term drama duet performance–pretty girl/plain girl. It’s quite touching and dramatic to recall my previous fear towards drama in the beginning of the term. I really need to be confident–always feel frustrated and anxious during rehearsal… oh I wouldn’t get out of my comfort zone! oh I’m acting so bad! oh tomorrow I would fail everyone! But I’m proved to be excellent! To my great surprise, nearly every peer was so amazed and shocked by my acting that they said ‘’You are already a superstar in Hollywood!” “You’ve completed a miracle!” Therefore I believe I really have done a great job. It is so amazing that I can exactly do those silly stuff that I dare not to imagine before. But what this experience impresses most is that it prove my hard work meaningful. All the frustration was not because of my really bad work, but the intention to behave perfect. Thanks to drama, I’ve been out of my comfort zone for so many times only in this term! I can conspicuously feel my great progress and developed confidence. Can you imagine that? I was even nervous during verbal presentation and felt so embarrassed when doing movements in front of the class. I think my present results greatly prove my great efforts–99% in GPA, 28/30 for pantomiming… Now I really feel confident in drama. It is not over confidence. It is my efforts that build up my confidence.
