BCA, My Future

hi，Mrs. Smith！sorry for disturbing you at night,but I’m just too exciting to tell you something Recently I’m taking some courses about my future major,and my teacher is in Oxford University…You know,I’m really really introverted and want to express so many things but just can’t help So you can’t imagine how thankful I am to you,to BCA! I talked to the teacher about dystopia and unwind, Macbeth and Shakespeare (I am reading his tales in advance).And when she mentioned Dickens,you can’t imagine how excited I was to shout out “Signalman!” What I found most challenging and makes me stressed out is the lady asked me to (pretend to)attend Oxford’s interview… but I did it and she said “well done”!You know why?It is because the close-reading interview in about to analyze a poem!She thought I might not know something like alliteration…and so surprisingly I shout out “Wow I know that !!! I also know it is written in narrative version or free verse…” Dear Mrs. Smith I suppose my major would definitely be related to comparative literature (I love English and Mandarin so much and want to be the connection between them)or education…no matter how,definitely about Arts. Before, I really doubted the necessity of learning these literacy knowledge,but now I know that that are exactly what I need for my major,for my future Because of what you’ve taught ,I can communicate with my teacher (maybe in the future my interviewer)so naturally and enthusiastically!!! The lady also mentioned that the interviewer cares more about your critical thinking and there’s no correct answer to any of the questions. That sounds so familiar…because you say that every time before class discussion!!! You are always my favorite teacher because you respect and mildly accept all my ideas… I think you are always helping me with my future invisibly… I can’t be more thankful… As aforementioned I am really shy [Lol]I force myself to raise my hand and that is the happiest thing I experienced in BCA! I’m too shy to show my emotions and I want you to know that I really really enjoy the novel unwind and can feel every single change of emotion of every character. Also for drama,so glad to learn soooo many new stuff helping me get out of comfort zone! I love everything you’ve taught…it’s just I’m scared to tell you because I think my poor English would bother you or makes you suffer…after all it must be very very hard to live abroad while facing so many poor English speakers all the day I really think you need to have more time relaxing so didn’t come to you… I also want to tell you another secret Initially I decided to go to A-level because I want to go to UK. But on the consulting day, our Chinese headmaster Mr.Zhang told me something, that made me change mind totally and chose a path I would never regret,that is: BCA is a real high school, with completed education system. IB and A-level are just only like after-school classes. So now you can understand how much I love BCA, love you…truly (sorry for typing out so many thoughts … I do have loads of thoughts despite being so quite [Lol]
