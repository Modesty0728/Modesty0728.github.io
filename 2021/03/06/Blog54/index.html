Lev's point of View Letter (second version)

​ Akron Ohio

​ December 4th, 2081

Dear family,

 Umm…hi…

​ Do you miss me? I suppose you still remember me…But to be honest, the eighteenth birthday of a tithe is not so significant right? After all you always told me: high God love you much more than we do.

I don’t know where to start, really. Papa, mama, you are clever. You had informed me my “holy duty” when I was so young and naive that I spontaneously believed everything you told. Gradually I blocked my fabulous imagination towards jobs and marriage, and my mind had nothing but “God is mighty”. Maybe I should be thankful for you specially give me a sheltered structure to grow up? Don’t you think it sounds ridiculous and familiar, like what Snape asks Dumbledore in their last talking: You’ve been raising Harry Potter like a pig for slaughter? I know, I know, irritably you would blurt out: how can you say that? The pig is nothing, but you are the direct treasure of holy God! Gosh how could you…My fault, you definitely are thinking in that way once I was born, and…I also find this one tricky. A few days ago, I told another unwind: living without purpose is dreadful and all that in my mind forcing me to turn him in is your voice— “why not “contribute a voice to the world” instead of being so meaningless?” But what’s your “holy” comprehension for “meaningless”? Uh, God! Because of your damn ideas, I hurt the boy, and myself! For thirteen years, you are the dimness of fire! I’m arrogant, exclusive, acting like cold-blooded animals, all blame on you. You still find yourself righteous? Read on. After I escaped from cops I called, I realize how much I want to be alive! In the past I was not alone due to the “glorious solace”, however now I am, indeed, separating myself from the identity of tithe. It is a brand-new feeling which I did not have, or in other words, should not have when being with you, like a fresh spring washing a headful of rust away. And for the first time I’m scared, conspicuously. Ha, levi, finally regretful? You wish. I want to be alive— totally alive. Eventually I start learning how to be a living person.

Curiously, how can loneliness, an emotion as cold as me, transform me and warm up my heart? Tell you then. Everyone is born with a warm heart, and gets company mostly. I’m envious about these companions in the likeness of forest fawns continuously transporting wood for the burning of soul, because care from you “saints” are just water vapor forming the dimness, so heavy actually, far beyond what I can burden. Indeed, you keep me company but what you will never realize is that the more you accompany me (actually brainwash me), the more I get to suffer, and my soul on fire is more and more invisible. Clearly, now you can see the different function of “wood” and “water vapor”.

​ I was stifling under water! But what else can I blames on you…You were fiercely protective towards me…maybe just as a tithe…I understand…I wouldn’t choose myself either…after all I cannot be more frustrated than now. Thirteen years of brainwashing…I will never forgive you…Have you ever respected my soul??? My soul comes to this world for living rather than any ridiculous destiny!

​ Nevertheless… am I correct…? Who is correct about this world? Pastor Dan? Me? You? Jesus…my brain is breaking into pieces… Maybe you are correct…maybe…If so, I become tiny water living in sponge while your “justice” is the hand squeezing me out… I have my entire body on your hand whereas will never be noticed and then go by the wind…uh then become “vapor” ha… a further step away from “myself”. Perhaps I’m just too selfish…or wouldn’t be so painful towards this…Is Pastor Dan doing the right thing—Let me run away? I don’t know but I do feel relieved…like being saved from death. So…let me be selfish…just for one time…okay? I haven’t found “myself” yet. I need more time definitely and I’m not in a hurry. But I do see the hazy figure of myself deep down. He wants to be alive, truly. Why so desirable? No idea. But please…this time let me follow my heart…and I won’t blame on you anymore…I need to figure out who I am. Others are just meaningless to me now.

After all, I haven’t loved myself for thirteen years.

​ Your TREASURE,

​ Lev Calder
