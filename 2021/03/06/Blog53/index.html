Lev's Point of View letter (first version)

​ Akron Ohio

​ December 4th, 2081

Dear family,

 Umm…hi…

​ Do you miss me? I suppose you still remember me…But to be honest, the eighteenth birthday of a tithe is not so significant right? After all you always told me: high God love you much more than we do.

​ I don’t know where to start, really, because you can never understand me due to thirteen years’ brainwashing. Ha, you may find it funny. How can it be brainwashing? Papa, mama, you are clever. You informed me my “holy duty” when I was so young thus naively believed everything you told. Gradually I locked my fabulous imagination, and my mind had nothing but “God is mighty”, “God creates us”, “God is worth sacrificing”. Maybe I should be thankful for you specially give me a sheltered structure to grow up? Don’t you think it sounds ridiculous and familiar, like what Snape asks Dumbledore in their last talking: You’ve been raising Harry Potter like a pig for slaughter? I know, I know, irritably you would blurt out: how can you say that? The pig is nothing, but you are the direct precious of holy God! So, do you think my life is meaningful? Oh, my fault, you definitely are thinking in that way once I was born. You know, I also find this question tricky. A few days ago, I told another unwind living without purpose is ridiculous and all that in my mind which make me turn him in is the “truth” you said to me: why not “contribute a voice to the world” instead of being so meaningless? But what’s your “holy” standard of “meaningless”? Uh, God, of course. Because of your damn ideas, I hurt the boy, and myself! For thirteen years, you are the dimness covers my hearts on fire. I’m arrogant, cold, and not compassionate, acting like cold-blooded animals, all blame on you. You still find yourself righteous right? Read on. After I escape from school, I realize how much I want to be alive! And how scary it is to face the possible death. In the past I’m not alone, since the “glorious solace” keeps me company. But now I’m alone. I’m separate myself from the identity of tithe, and I begin to fear. It is a brand-new feeling which I did not have, or in other words, should not have when being with you. Ha, levi, finally regretful? You wish. Now I find myself and my heart. I want to be alive, totally alive. Eventually I start learning how to be a living person. Curiously, how can loneliness, an emotion as cold as me transform me and warm up my heart? Tell you then. Everyone is born with a warm heart, and everyone gets company. I’m envious about these companions in the likeness of forest fawn continuously transport wood for burning, for all of you “saints” are amounts of water vapor leading to dimness, so heavy actually, far beyond what I can burden. Indeed you keep me company but what you will never realize is that the more you accompany me (brainwash me), the more I get to suffer, stepping away from my heart further and further until the fire is totally invisible. Now I think you do understand the difference between wood and water vapor.

​ Do you know how stifling my life is? I’m like a fish trapped beneath ice! But what else can I blames on you…You have been fiercely protective towards me as a tithe…is it only because of my identity or myself? I know…You choose to protect your faith…I wouldn’t choose myself either…after all I cannot be more frustrated than I feel now. Thirteen years’ brainwash…I will never forgive you…Have you ever respected my soul??? My soul comes to this world for living rather than any ridiculous destiny!

​ But… am I correct…? Who is correct about this world? Pastor Dan? Me? You? Jesus…my brain is breaking into pieces! Maybe you are correct…maybe…If so, I’m tiny water living in sponge while your “justice” is the hand squeezing me out… I have my entire body on you but will never be noticed and then go by the wind…uh then become vapor ha… a further step away from “myself”. Perhaps I’m just too selfish…or wouldn’t be so painful for this…Is Pastor Dan doing the right thing—Let me run away? I don’t know but I do feel relieved…like being saved in front of death. So…let me be selfish…just for one time…okay? I haven’t myself yet since I need more time definitely and I’m not in a hurry. But I do see the hazy figure of myself deep down. He wants to be alive, truly. Why so serious? No idea. But please…this time let me follow my heart…and I won’t blame on you anymore…I need to find out who I am. Others are now meaningless to me.

After all, I haven’t loved myself for thirteen years.

​ Your TREASURE,

​ Lev Calder
