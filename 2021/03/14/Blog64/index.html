Character's Physicality ( for Scarlett O'Hara)

Head, Face, & Neck

l Expressiveness

I can exactly use the depiction from the original to describe Scarlett in the movie— “The green eyes in the carefully sweet face were turbulent, willful, lusty with life, distinctly at variance with her decorous demeanor.” Scarlett has got a pair of eyes for story-telling, and has so much life that she rolls eyeballs frequently and her eyebrows are always dancing. The pursing of lips and exuded “grievance” in face both prove Scarlett a “skilled flirt”. Generally, Scarlett always wears exaggerated, lovely, and piteous expressions which fills men with protectiveness towards her.

l Make and appreciate eye contact.

Scarlett is often over-confident and remains ambivalent towards men. The reason Scarlett is so attractive to them is that her lovely green eyes can took men’s soul which is like a hook attaching to their heart. Imagine such a charming girl are gazing at you while offering an arresting grin.

l Tend to smile, but then smirk

Before the break out of Civil War, Scarlett’s smile is naïve, genuine, and flirtatious. However, having gone through parents’ passing away and several bad-ending marriages, she is just like the flower flying in the wind, catering to the request of world and life. The facial is not as lively as before: the size of smile is gradually fixed.

Upper body:

l Upright posture, like a princess

Scarlett always has her arms bent in front of body to lift dress with head lordly raised up, which is almost the same compared with the depiction from the original— “The green eyes in the carefully sweet face were turbulent, willful, lusty with life, distinctly at variance with her decorous demeanor.”

l Active gestures & touches

When meeting men, Scarlett always offer her hands directly for a kiss and sometimes point at them saucily.

l Extremely shallow & deep breaths

Normally, Scarlett looks as if she is not breathing at all, which may benefit from the good education as a lady. However, it is also very apparent that stress or fear can also intensify her breaths greatly. In this case, Scarlett acts as if she will suffer a split-second faint.

Lower body:

l light on her feet

As Melanie says, “Oh Scarlett, you have so much life.” Scarlett is light-footed like a little cat. However, she stumbles from the stairs later, which leads to a miscarriage.

l Quick and saucy movements

Scarlett always acts as if she is in a rush to a ball. But later on, I realized that she gets a short temper which I associate with pepper.t

l Walk heel-first

Although Scarlett is always releasing her charm intentionally, she seems full of confidence and her steps are full of strength. Clearly she is a remarkably adroit and determined woman.
