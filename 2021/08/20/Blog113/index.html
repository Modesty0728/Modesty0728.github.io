amazing experience at HSYLC2021

Before applying for HSYLC, I did not consider about the reason why I wanted to spend ten days on it. When I started to browse its website, I found out that this summer camp is associated with the part that I was never familiar with, i.e. liberal arts. And it was the the first time that I was so deeply attracted and loosened the determination of going for UK universities. And HSYLC is about leadership in majority, which was also one of the parts that I never touched. So I just decided to do an experience, and go out of my comfort zone again.

In my great surprise, I received the most exciting news of this year– I was accepted by HSYLC! Then came to the most exciting part– selecting seminars/capstone projects/frontier conversations/info sessions… I did want to immerse myself into my literary major world. But I knew, that I came here, to go out of my comfort zone, and to experience liberal arts. So I did the most excellent action this year: I chose only one seminar based on my major. And for the rest, I tried what is wholly out of interests: leadership, nutrition, linguistics, Arab. I was so exhilarated since it was the first step of understanding what is liberal arts, truly.

In autumn, I tried to find resources in order to take the place of HSYLC seminars due to the pervasive virus, but feel so lucky to be involved in again to gain loads of skills that unexpectedly emerged during daily interactions.

I learned systematically about Tang shi and Song ci. At the end of the day, there are so many rules the ancient needed to follow! Being plagued by those fixed rhythm, I know that I still have a long way to go in order to become proficient in my country’s culture.
